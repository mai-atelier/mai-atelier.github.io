Contrast

Notion

notion.so

I think the notion website has beautiful contrasts with fonts and colors, making a visual hierarchy. The fonts are the same typeface, but it is different in sizes and font colors with grayscale. The page has a clear contrast of white background and black elements. The bold tagline pulls the user's eyes quickly with the image, also black and white. The accent color for the call-to-action button is greyish red contrasting with the white fonts, so the button looks different from other elements to grab the attention. The site also contrasts with their style of using emoji in triad colors. These small portions of elements with colors making the look sophisticated. The example view of the service in the bottom image with box shadows looking different from the above elements.
